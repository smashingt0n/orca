Notes on this data
  • Power Destroyed stats are excluded (source data was corrupted) — kills only.
  • A player's "baseline" is their kill count the first time they appear on any roster snapshot; players who joined after May 1 have a later baseline date.
  • All 5 snapshots (including Jul 10) are matched by unique player ID/link, so alliance switches and renames are tracked reliably. A stray corrupted row in the original "Orca Baseline PVP Kills" sheet (a player-link URL that had leaked into the name column) was filtered out — it had been showing up as a phantom "left" entry.
  • The Jul 10 snapshot is the last full roster pull (taken Jul 8) with a handful of specifically-reported roster changes layered on top — not a fresh full re-pull of everyone. Only players with a confirmed update (a join, departure, or alliance switch) differ from their Jul 8 reading; everyone else's kill count is exactly what it was on Jul 8.
  • Manually reported roster events folded into the Jul 10 snapshot: Dagger7 switched Cerb→Orca (7/9, new alliance baseline of 21,311 kills); ValRHarding joined Orca fresh (7/9, baseline 1,978 kills); Awesomedude5927 and USSENTERPRISE9583 left Orca for the Voy alliance (7/9); KJonEarth left Cerb (7/10). CapHarriman, Stinkeybudy420 (Orca) and Steino (Cerb) were added to the leadership list. Exact departure dates for existing casualties were backfilled where they could be matched, by exact stfc.pro ID or name/alias, against two alliance-history files covering Orca and Cerb departures.
  • CaptainMikeSparrow (Cerb) joined and left within a few days sometime during the war, entirely between two of our snapshots — no kill data was ever captured for them. They're still shown on the Cerb casualty list with a "no data" tag for completeness.
  • 3 confirmed Cerb→Orca switchers (by player ID): Sivem, SpartanSardaukar (recently renamed SpartanMultiPass), and Aiter (renamed twice: AiterGetsGrumpy → AiterOnTheOcean → AiterBigBaddaBoom). Their alliance changed sometime in the Jun 1→Jun 16 window; since only snapshot totals are available, that window's kill gain is split 50/50 between Cerb and Orca for aggregate totals.
  • Leadership list (25 players) comes from the "Leadership Player IDs" sheet, unchanged across all 4 comparator files. Use the Roster Table's "Leadership" view to compare the two leaderships side by side.
  • The Roster Table and Player Explorer both default to the Orca tab; switch to Cerb or All alongside either. Top 30 and Leadership views always show both alliances side by side for comparison.
  • Casualties = players present at the "From" snapshot who are no longer present at the "To" snapshot. Kill counts shown on the Casualties tab are total war kills gained from baseline through their last snapshot in the alliance (not lifetime totals, and not just kills-at-the-"From"-date). Destinations were checked by fetching each departed player's live stfc.pro profile and reading their current alliance — "Went to X" for a real transfer (including back into Orca/Cerb, which usually means a gap in the source snapshot data rather than a true departure), or "Not in an Alliance" if their profile shows none. This is a point-in-time check, not continuously updated.
